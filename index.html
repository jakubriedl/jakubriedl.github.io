My beloved passions

Hello everybody from Prague, Czech Republic. This website is about my passions. Greatest one is IT which become my job too. I started as administrator and I quickly hit the top. I was building private cloud for one Czech factory. After that I focused more to clever systems and founded start-up Softsolution with Softaukce software for saving company costs. In addition to this project I started huge challenge developing brand new system for LEO Express. That led me to become head of IT department. In 2014 we merged with new start-up called Bileto where I am team leader of B2B team.

From the real world I love delicious food and drink. Italian, Chinese and Czech cuisine are my favourite. To the food I love distinctive wines (eg. Chardonnay, Merlot, Champagne) or Czech beer. And this all fits nice with my passion to golf, football and motor sport.
